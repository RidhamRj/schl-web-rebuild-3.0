About The School

MAPS-A NEXGEN SCHOOL is managed by Agarwal Welfare Society stemming from high ideals of socialism and economic equality had its genesis in the vision of the great saint Maharaja Agarsainji. With the visions 'EMPOWERED MINDS AND ENERGISED SOULS', the school aims at inspiring the young minds and souls to explore and be unstoppable creative innovators with independent thinking ,who turn their knowledge into application and are grounded to Indian values prepared to succeed globally.
